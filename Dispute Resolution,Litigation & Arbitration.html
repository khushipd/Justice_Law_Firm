DISPUTE RESOLUTION,LITIGATION & ARBITRATION

::after

We have built a robust practice in Dispute Resolution anchoring upon its vast experience, established expertise and diverse capabilities in handling complex and multi- jurisdictional corporate and commercial disputes through litigation, arbitration, conciliation and mediation. We are known as a redoubtable name in dispute resolution in regulatory, commercial and corporate matters. The firm handles matters not only in locations where it has its own offices, but also in other cities across India, where it has developed alliances and trusting relationships with high calibre law firms and professionals to deliver impeccable solutions to our clients.

Litigation Services provided by us include Drafting and filing of representations, applications, pleadings, Suits, Petitions Writ Petitions, Special Leave Petitions, defending cases against our clients in the various courts and tribunals including District Courts, NCLT, NCLAT, DRT, DRAT, National Green Tribunal, Consumer Forums, Competition Commission & its Appellate Tribunal, SEBI, SAT, AERAT, High Courts, The Supreme Court of India etc and representing our clients before the said Courts, Tribunals and authorities.
